Our Topic

In 2018-2019 almost half a million teachers (and educational staff) have protested or participated in strikes across the United States. Many of these protests/strikes were related to the #RedforEd movement. The demands varied by state but highlighted teacherâ€™s dissatisfaction with compensation, funding, working conditions and much more. This project analyzes U.S. education data to understand the underlying conditions that have led to statewide protests/strikes in 7 states: Arizona, Colorado, Kentucky, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina, Virginia, and West Virginia.
